Introducing a new neighborhood cafe

Hello Smyrna-Clayton,
The Skyline Café
will be coming soon!!!

New from Priceless Confections is The Skyline Café!

Yes, we did it. We put together a new restaurant called The Skyline Café which is specially designed to provide fresh chef-inspired menus with healthy choices as well as some local favorites.

We are planning to have breakfast and lunch menus with daily specials and we have options for everyone's lifestyle including dishes that are vegan, vegetarian, keto, and more!

Look for your invitation for coffee when the cafe opens in early 2021 in the Smyrna Health & Wellness Center in the heart of downtown Smyrna. This location will have baked goods from Priceless Confections and we will be offering full service catering.

Who we are...

In 2012, Liz Hansen began Priceless Confections as a way to give back to the Smyrna-Clayton community with wonderful cakes and cupcakes. In recent years this was broadend into a full service catering team and now we are expanding into a centralized venue in the historic district of Smyrna Delaware.

Chef Jeff is here too!

Jeff Moore has joined the team full time for the expansion into the cafe and we look forward to the grand opening coming soon! Jeff started his culinary chops at Johnson and Wales University focused in Culinary Arts and Chef Training. Be sure and follow us on your favorite social media platform.
