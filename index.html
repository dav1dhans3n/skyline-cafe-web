Recent News

Mar 18, 2021 Thank you to the Central Delaware Chamber of Commerce and all of our supporters that have helped The Skyline Café open the doors and begin operations. The CDCC hosted a ribbon cutting ceremony on March 18, 2021, in the Smyrna Health and Wellness Center at 100 South Main Street, Suite 102, Smyrna, Delaware.

The Skyline Café is a collaboration between Elizabeth Hansen and Chef Jeff Moore to open a restaurant that offers an appetizing variety of healthy breakfast and lunch options made from scratch and prepared fresh daily, directly on the premises with respect to customers preferences and at an excellent price.

The Skyline Café includes treasured recipes from many of the great cities across the USA as well as some local favorites. Come see us and you will find the difference that using fresh ingredients makes.
