Recent updates

Feb 20, 2021 Elizabeth Hansen, founder of Priceless Confections announced today that The Skyline Café has opened the doors and will have a ribbon cutting ceremony on March 18, 2021, in the Smyrna Health and Wellness Center at 100 South Main Street, Suite 102, Smyrna, Delaware. The Skyline Café is a collaboration between Liz and Chef Jeff Moore, one of Delaware's up and coming favorite chefs.

These two Delaware Natives heard their inner voices urging them to open a restaurant that offers an appetizing variety of healthy breakfast and lunch options made from scratch and prepared fresh daily, directly on the premises with respect to customers preferences and at an excellent price.

As a result, The Skyline Café was born and includes treasured recipes from many of the great cities across the USA as well as some local favorites. Come see us and you will find the difference that using fresh ingredients makes.
