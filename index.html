May 25, 2021

Did you ever notice the term "eco-friendly" gets thrown around a lot? It appears on everything from flooring to kitchen utensils. Here at The Skyline Cafe, we understand the importance of an eco-friendly lifestyle and work to source our products and supplies so they are safe for the environment.

For example, our coffee cups and food containers are made of all natural, compostable and biodegradeble products with no binders or chemicals. When they land in a landfill, they naturally biodegrade in less than 2 months.


For catering events, we can provide trendy dinnerware like this plate made from Palm leaves which are 100% natural and sustainable, biodegradeable, compostable, microwave safe, and water resistant.
