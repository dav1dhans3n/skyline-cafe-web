Catering

Skyline Catering is a family-owned and operated company started on good family values, and a desire to give back to our community with a personalized approach to clients. Our focus is on preparing and serving exceptional food along with some of the most tempting desserts. No event is too big or too small for us to cater. Our hope is to make your event so memorable that we are the first ones you think of when planning your next event.

Liz and Chef Jeff work with events of all sizes and can offer both full service on-site, and drop-off catering options. We are passionate about providing perfectly balanced flavors to your meals, and the right amount of taste and sweetness to your baked goods.

Skyline Catering has created various brochures of "Packages" and "Add-Ons" that allow you to customize your menu to best suit the needs of your guests. We believe our packages will give you the freedom to select and create your own unique menu for your special day. With our outstanding cuisine, attention to detail, and our excellent customer service, together we can make your events extraordinary and memorable for you and your guests.

Preferred Vendor Locations

  • Del Agricultural Museum and Village (Dover, DE)
  • Del State Troopers Association (Dover, DE)
  • Jonathan's Landing (Magnolia, DE)
  • King Cole Farm (Dover, DE)
  • Loblolly Acres (Woodside, DE)
  • Vanderwende Acres (Seaford, DE)
  • Willowbrook (Bridgeville, DE)

What does it mean to be a preferred vendor? A preferred vendor is just that, preferred by a venue that provides event space for weddings, meetings, and other celebrations. You are reasonably assured that they will provide high quality services for your event. This will also save you time as you begin to plan your event because they already know the location and specifics of the venue, and this will streamline the setup for your celebration.
