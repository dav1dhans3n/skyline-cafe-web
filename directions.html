Directions

The Skyline Cafe is in Suite 102 on the first floor of the Smyrna Health and Wellness Center at 100 South Main Street in Smyrna, Delaware.
Our chef inspired dishes are prepared using the freshest finest ingredients. Come in for quick service or order online for ultimate convenience.

Hours of Operation

Monday - Friday: 7am - 3pm and Saturdays: 7am - Noon
